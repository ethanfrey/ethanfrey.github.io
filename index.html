Securing the chain #1: PoW style
16 December 2016

The biggest win of blockchain technology is security. It is argued that the technology allows one to take many computer, a number possibly insecure or malicious, and weave them together into a secure system, harder to hack into than a bank. This is largely true, as you add more machines to the blockchain, you don’t scale capacity, but rather security.
